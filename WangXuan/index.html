Biography

Received a B.E. degree in engineering of Surveying and Mapping (ESM) from China University of Mining and Technology, Xuzhou, China in 2018.

Received a master's degree in engineering degree in Engineering of Geodesy and Survey (EGS) from China University of Mining and Technology, Xuzhou, China in 2021.

He is currently pursuing a Ph.D. degree at Wuhan University, Wuhan, China.

Research Interests

Research interests include Integrated Navigation, Indoor Positioning, Seamless indoor and outdoor positioning. The main research focus on Integrated Navigation.
